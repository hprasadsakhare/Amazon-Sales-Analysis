- **Category Leadership:** Men Shoes dominates with $9.40M YTD sales (43.18% market share)

- **Seasonal Growth:** Q4 performance shows $811.09K QTD vs $2.18M YTD indicating strong holiday season impact
- **Review Engagement:** 19.42M reviews demonstrate high customer engagement across product categories
